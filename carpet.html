Our Process


At Ace Carpet and Upholstry Cleaning, we use machine mounted equiptment to steam clean carpets and upholstry. We run hoses from our machine into the building and spend between 40 minutes to 3 hours steam cleaning the carpets depending on the size. Setup time usually takes about 10-15 minutes, and owner presence is recommended to discuss expectations and discuss the process further. Dry time is assisted with commercial grade fans used during the process.

Rvs, Campers, Area Carpets and other small jobs typically only take 1-2 hours to complete.

Dry Time


With the steam cleaning process, there is a window of dry time needed for the process. We recommend leaving the carpets to air dry overnight, without the disturbance of foot traffic walking on the carpets. This time does vary based on the season and type of carpet. To assist with this process, we often use commercial grade fans to speed the drying time and depending on the situation, will leave the fans there overnight and return the next day to pick them up.
