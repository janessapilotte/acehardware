Our Process


Using advanced machine attached equiptment, we clean tile and grout. The steam from the process lifts the dirt up without leaving dirt or chemical residue behind to resettle back into the grout. The video also only shows a simple first round of cleaning, tile and grout is treated with multiple rounds of cleaning to ensure that the work done is quality.
